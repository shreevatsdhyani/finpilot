# Code of Conduct

## Our standard

We are committed to a welcoming, harassment-free community for everyone,
regardless of background, identity, or experience level.

## Expected behavior

1. Be respectful and constructive.
2. Assume good intent and give helpful feedback.
3. Focus discussions on the code and ideas.

## Unacceptable behavior

1. Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks
2. Trolling or intentionally disruptive conduct
3. Sharing private information without permission

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing this code of
conduct and may remove, edit, or reject contributions that violate it.
